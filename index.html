How It Works
Honest guide — what this tool does, what it can't do, and how to get the most out of it
01
Set your profile once
Go to Profile → fill your name, skills, summary. AI uses this to score job matches and personalise every cover letter with YOUR actual achievements.
02
Fetch jobs by keyword
Go to Hunt → pick job boards → click Fetch. AI generates realistic current job listings AND opens each board in a new tab with your keywords pre-filtered to last 7 days.
03
Apply in 10 seconds
Click Apply on any job → AI generates cover letter, Gmail-ready email, LinkedIn message, and follow-up. One click opens Gmail with subject and body pre-filled.
04
Paste any JD
Find a job on Naukri/LinkedIn. Copy the description. Go to Apply Engine → Paste JD → AI regenerates everything tailored to that exact post. Works for ANY job from ANY site.
⚠ What this tool can't do (and why)
Cannot auto-submit on Naukri/LinkedIn: Job boards block automated submissions. Any tool claiming to do this will get your account banned. The best automation is: AI handles ALL the writing instantly — you click Submit on the job board (takes 10 seconds).

Job listings aren't from live scraping: They're AI-generated based on your keywords and are used for match scoring. The real search happens when you click the source links — those open actual live job boards with your filters applied.
